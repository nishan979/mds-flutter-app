# Videos

Place your splash screen video here.

**Required file:** `splash.mp4` - This video will be played on the splash screen.

The video will play once, then wait 2 seconds before navigating to the language selection page.

**Recommended specifications:**

- Format: MP4
- Duration: 3-5 seconds
- Resolution: 1080x1920 (portrait) or your preferred aspect ratio
- File size: Keep under 5MB for faster loading
